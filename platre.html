Célia Chen

Plaster: The Parsley 2022

For the exhibition Statues modèles, Une histoire de l'enseignement artistique à Amiens (A history of artistic education in Amiens) at the Musée de Picardie, Amiens;
I made a representation of the plaster of the Parsley Leaf, fragmented under several angles, in a range of bright colors, in abstract and differentiated compositions.
